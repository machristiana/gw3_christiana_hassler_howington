Gentlemen of the Ton

Meet the Gentlemen

The patriarchy reigns supreme in the ton as the men control the money and their families. While many of these gentle forge their own path in life, they are still bound to follow the rules of etiquette. Sometimes, the unspoken rules of conduct lead to great pain. I predict that in the end, these young gentlemen will find what they’re looking for in life.

"From the mornings you ease, to the evenings you quiet, to the dreams you inhabit… my thoughts of you never end."

Simon Basset, Duke

A mysterious suitor with a troubled childhood, Simon seems to be the center of every party. With no interest in finding a wife, he soon becomes enthralled in a heated relationship with Daphne Bridgerton.

"I love you with everything I am, everything I've been, and everything I hope to be. I love you with my past, and I love you for my future."

Colin Bridgerton

The third oldest of the Bridgerton family, Colin has little pressure to find a wife at this time. From attending the parties and being a man of great honor, Colin became a sought after bachelor by Penelope and Marina.

"Must our only options be to squawk and settle or to never leave the nest?"

- Eloise Bridgerton

"A man of any honor ensures his debts are fully paid."

Anthony Bridgerton, Viscount

The eldest son of the Bridgerton family, and head of the family, Anthony takes his job seriously. When his father died at 18, he took on the responsibilities of the family and in particular, finding a suitor for Daphne. Besides turning men away from his sister, he’s also known as the town rake.

"I cannot help it. You are perfection itself."

Prince Friedrich

In London to find a wife, Prince Friedrich has taken a fancy to Daphne. For a time, they looked as if there would be a wedding between the two. Over his aunt’s, the Queen, objections, he returns to Prussia to recover from heartbreak.
